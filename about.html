About Bhashabridge Consultants

Language solutions built on accuracy, trust, and professionalism.

Bhashabridge Consultants is a professional language translation service provider dedicated to delivering accurate, confidential, and culturally precise translations. We specialize in English–French and English–Japanese translation, in both directions.

We work with individuals on personal documents, academic certificates, and professional profiles, and with organizations on corporate, legal, and technical translation under NDA-based confidentiality. Alongside translation, we also run beginner French classes (A1–A2) with a grammar and conversation focus.
