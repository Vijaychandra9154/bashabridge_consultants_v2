Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before requesting a translation or joining a French class.

Which languages do you translate between?

We specialize in French–English and Japanese–English translation, in both directions.

Do you provide certified or attested translations?

Requirements vary by document and by the institution requesting it. Tell us what the translation is for when you request a quote and we'll confirm whether certification or attestation is needed and possible.

How long does a translation take?

Turnaround depends on document length, subject complexity, and language pair. Share your document with us for an estimated delivery timeline.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing depends on word count, language pair, subject complexity, and formatting requirements. Contact us with your document for a free quote.

Is my document kept confidential?

Yes. All documents are handled confidentially, and NDA-based agreements are available for corporate and bulk projects.

Do you handle legal, technical, or academic documents?

Yes, alongside personal document translation we handle academic transcripts, corporate content, and legal and technical documents.

How do I request a quote?

Use the quote form on the Services or Contact page, email us directly, or message us on WhatsApp with your document details.

Do you offer French classes?

Yes, we offer beginner French classes (A1–A2) with a grammar and conversation focus, and flexible scheduling.
